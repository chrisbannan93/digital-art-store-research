# PDP Gallery Requirements Checklist (v1 Nursery Printable Kits)

## Global Rules (all SKU types)
- [ ] Gallery order follows the exact sequence below; no reordering.
- [ ] Images use consistent lighting tone across full set.
- [ ] Room mockups are nursery-relevant and non-AI-styled for v1.
- [ ] Frame style is consistent within a single SKU gallery.
- [ ] Angles and crop style are consistent and calm (no dramatic distortion).
- [ ] Editing is natural and premium (no oversaturation, no harsh filters).
- [ ] Text overlays remain minimal and readable.
- [ ] Any trust/policy strip stays non-intrusive.

---

## Singles Gallery (7-8 images, fixed order)

### 1) Nursery hero mockup
- **Caption intent:** Show finished-wall outcome immediately.
- **Anxiety resolved:** “Will this actually look good in a nursery?”

### 2) Detail crop (quality proof)
- **Caption intent:** Show line clarity/texture at close range.
- **Anxiety resolved:** “Will the print look blurry or low quality?”

### 3) What’s included graphic
- **Caption intent:** Snapshot of files included (ZIP, 5 ratios, guide).
- **Anxiety resolved:** “What exactly am I getting?”

### 4) `Download -> Print -> Frame` panel
- **Caption intent:** Show process simplicity in one panel.
- **Anxiety resolved:** “Is this process complicated?”

### 5) Size/ratio chart
- **Caption intent:** Clarify frame-size compatibility.
- **Anxiety resolved:** “Will it fit my frame sizes?”

### 6) `PRINT_ME_FIRST` preview
- **Caption intent:** Demonstrate test-print confidence tool.
- **Anxiety resolved:** “How do I avoid wasting paper/money?”

### 7) Secondary lifestyle angle
- **Caption intent:** Show style flexibility in another nursery setting.
- **Anxiety resolved:** “Will this still work in my room style?”

### 8) Optional support/license strip
- **Caption intent:** Quiet reassurance on help + personal-use terms.
- **Anxiety resolved:** “What if I get stuck or misuse files?”

- [ ] Color balance matches across all gallery assets for SKU.
- [ ] White point and exposure are consistent image-to-image.
- [ ] Frame proportions accurately reflect ratio examples.
- [ ] No contradictory claims between gallery text and PDP copy.
- [ ] Digital-only wording is accurate wherever referenced.
- [ ] Trio assets include layout suggestion visual (required for Trio).
- [ ] Singles and Trio galleries each meet required image count range.
