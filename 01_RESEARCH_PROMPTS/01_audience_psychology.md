# 01_audience_psychology

---
## Copy/paste below into ChatGPT (Deep Research recommended)
---

You are acting as a **market research analyst** conducting **evidence-based discovery** for a new digital-first printable wall art store.

Where possible:
- Ground insights in **observed buyer behaviour**, not just theory
- Reference **current trends, examples, or patterns** from:
  - ecommerce
  - paid social ads
  - creator content
  - marketplaces (Etsy, Shopify, etc.)
- Clearly distinguish:
  - what is **well-established**
  - what is a **reasonable hypothesis**
- Do **not** invent statistics; qualitative evidence and patterns are acceptable

## Output Format (follow strictly)
Return your response using exactly these sections in order:
1. **Summary (5 bullets max)**
2. **Deep insights (detailed)**
3. **Decisions I should make now**
4. **What to put into CONTEXT_PACK (10 bullets max)**
5. **Open questions / risks**
6. **Next actions (checklist)**

Store recommendations for a **digital-first printable wall art store on Shopify**, optimized for **paid social ads** now, with **optional future print-on-demand** expansion.

Assume niche is **TBD**; output should propose **3 niche directions** based on buyer motivations.

When proposing niches:
- Explain *why* they are likely to convert early
- Indicate which ad platforms they are best suited for (Meta vs TikTok)
- Indicate whether buyers are impulse-driven or deliberative
